Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the PowerHub 1500

How long does it take to charge the PowerHub 1500?

With the included 200W solar panel in optimal sunlight conditions, the PowerHub 1500 can be fully charged in approximately 6-8 hours. Using AC power from the grid, it takes about 4-5 hours for a complete charge.

Can I run my refrigerator on the PowerHub 1500?

Yes, the PowerHub 1500 can power most standard refrigerators (up to 1000W) for 4-6 hours. However, for energy-efficient refrigerators (400-600W), you can expect 8-10 hours of runtime.

Is the PowerHub 1500 waterproof?

The PowerHub 1500 has an IP54 rating, which means it's protected against dust and water splashes. However, it should not be exposed to heavy rain or submerged in water.

How many charge cycles can the battery handle?

The lithium-ion battery in the PowerHub 1500 is rated for over 1000 charge cycles while maintaining at least 80% of its original capacity. With typical usage, this translates to approximately 3-5 years of reliable service.

Is there a mobile app to monitor the PowerHub 1500?

Yes, the PowerHub 1500 can be monitored via our free mobile app available for both Android and iOS. The app allows you to check battery status, power input/output, and estimated runtime remaining.
